## Overview

Built and led a team of seven engineers shipping a sub-second analytics product.
Owned architecture, hiring, and customer-facing engineering.

## Highlights

- Scaled ingest from 1k to 250k events/second on the same infra footprint.
- Drove p99 query latency from 3.2s to 180ms over six months.
- Migrated the platform to multi-region active-active without downtime.

## Lessons Learned

The best engineering decisions look obvious in retrospect. Most of them came
from talking to customers, not from looking at dashboards.
