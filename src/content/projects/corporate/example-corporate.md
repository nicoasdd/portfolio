## Overview

Designed and led a two-year migration of a Fortune-500 employer's identity
platform, replacing a 15-year-old SSO with a modern OIDC implementation. Worked
across security, platform, and 30+ application teams.

## Highlights

- Zero unplanned outage during the rollout.
- Reduced sign-in latency by 60% on average.
- Authored the internal SSO integration guide that became the company-wide
  standard.

## Lessons Learned

Migrations are 20% engineering and 80% communication. The architectural decisions
were straightforward; the rollout planning was where the real work happened.
