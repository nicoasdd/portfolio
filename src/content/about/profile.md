## Hi, I'm Your Name

I design and build web products that are quick, considered, and a pleasure to use. Most of my work sits at the seam between **engineering and design** — turning fuzzy product ideas into shipped, accessible interfaces that hold up under real traffic.

Over the past several years I've worked across **personal side projects**, **early-stage startups**, and **established product teams**. The shape of the team changes; the values don't: ship something small that works, then make it sharper. Treat performance and accessibility as features, not afterthoughts. Write code your future self can read.

### What I care about

- **Craft** — typography, motion, and interaction details that quietly make a product feel _right_.
- **Performance** — pages that load on a flaky 3G connection, not just on a fibre desk.
- **Accessibility** — every visitor, every device, every input modality. WCAG AA is the floor, not the ceiling.
- **Pragmatism** — the simplest tool that solves the problem, not the most fashionable one.
