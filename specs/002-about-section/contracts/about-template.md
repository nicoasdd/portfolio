## Hi, I'm {Your Name}

Write the long-form version of your story here using normal Markdown.

A good About body usually covers:

- **Who you are** — a paragraph that humanizes you beyond the headline.
- **What you do** — the kinds of problems you solve and the contexts you've worked in (startups, agencies, in-house teams).
- **What you care about** — engineering values, design sensibilities, the things that make your work _yours_.
- **What you're looking for** — the kind of role, project, or collaboration you'd love to find next.

You can use **bold**, _italics_, `code`, lists, and links freely. Keep it warm but professional — recruiters and clients should finish reading and feel they know who they're hiring.
